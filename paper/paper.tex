\begin{abstract}
We evaluate the effects of the reinstated ABAWD work requirement policy in Michigan using a county-month panel (2016--2019). We use the Callaway--Sant'Anna estimator for staggered DiD, DDD (adult vs.\ child), and report event-study and robustness results. Heterogeneity is analyzed via DML; we also provide forecast and risk outputs.
\end{abstract}
